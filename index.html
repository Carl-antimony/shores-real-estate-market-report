What the data says
A 2-person office outperforming teams with 10× the headcount
Shores Real Estate produces $5.14M in combined office GCI with just two active agents. LJ Hooker Avalon Beach has 10 agents generating $15.16M — that's $1.52M per agent. Shores runs at $2.57M per agent. On a per-agent efficiency basis, Shores is 69% more productive than the dominant local franchise. That is not a small office — that is a high-performance business operating inside a boutique structure.
28-day DOM in a market where the independent average is 64
The biggest commercial argument in this region is the 26-day DOM gap between independent and franchise operators. Shores is not part of that gap — running at 28 days, matching franchise-tier clearance rates. That level of execution from a boutique signals strong vendor relationships and campaign quality. The question is whether their brand infrastructure is doing enough to attract the vendors who don't yet know them.

Movement 3  ·  The Gap
What the data is telling us
Antimony InsightWhat we see that others miss
Shores is operating at a level most independents in Sydney never reach. But three specific signals in the data point to a structural ceiling — and a clear pathway through it.
⚡Unfair Advantage
Stephanie is the only principal-led independent in the top 5 of this market — achieving near-franchise GCI with none of the franchise overhead, fee-sharing, or brand dilution. She is selling faster (29 days) than every agent ranked above her. That combination — speed, independence and personal accountability — is genuinely irreplicable by any franchise in this area. Vendors who understand it will choose Shores every time. The challenge is reaching the ones who don't know to look for it yet.
✦Silver Bullet
The DOM story is Shores' single most powerful commercial asset — and it's almost entirely invisible in the market right now. 29 days versus a 64-day independent average and a 38-day franchise average is a result most vendors would choose a major brand to get. Shores is already delivering it. Activating this one number — consistently, visually, across every vendor touchpoint — is the highest-leverage move available.
🎯Low-Hanging Fruit
Shores already achieves a $3.1M median on house sales specifically. The overall $2.59M median is being pulled down by unit and apartment transactions. A targeted strategy to attract more $3M+ house listings in Avalon and Newport — without changing transaction volume — would move both the median price and GCI materially within one listing cycle. No new infrastructure required. Just deliberate positioning.
